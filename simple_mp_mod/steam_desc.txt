A Simple Multiplayer Mod which retains vanilla balance and cosmetics, while adding minor features and quality of life improvements.

[h2]Version [b]1.0.1[/b] | Checksum [b]ec48[/b][/h2]

[h2]Mod Features:[/h2]
[list]
[*]Removed American Natives (except in the Mexico and Peru Regions).
[*]Nations with a European capital in the western technology group gain a free colonist at tech 7.
[/list]

[h2]Quality of Life Changes:[/h2]
[list]
[*]Decision to set all state edicts.
[*]Decision to remove destroy all buildings of a type.
[*]Decision to add all provinces to parliament.
[/list]

This mod [b]does[/b] affect the checksum, however it will be compatible with most cosmetic mods.
